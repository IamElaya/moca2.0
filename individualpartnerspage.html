About Us

ABC Auto Body is a family owned business that opened its doors in 1984 in the east side of Vancouver, B.C. with big hopes and dreams of making a big impact in the competitive auto industry. Our body shop has now become a successful and trusted auto collision shop by insurance companies and its customers in the Metro Vancouver area.
